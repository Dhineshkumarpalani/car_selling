FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions on Maruti Suzuki Xl6



1. Which are the variants available from Maruti XL6?

The BS6 compliant Maruti XL6 is currently available in 4 variants. All the variants have a petrol option only. Maruti XL6 is powered with a K15B petrol engine combined with smart hybrid technology and providing a 103.2 bhp at 6000 rpm and an ARAI mileage of 19.01 kmpl for all variants. The variants are XL6 Zeta, XL6 Alpha, Xl6 Zeta AT and Alpha AT with an ex-showroom price of about 9.86, 10.43, 10.97 and 11.54 lakhs respectively. To know more about XL6, visit your nearest Maruti showroom and experience the XL6 power and elegance.

2. What are the dimensions of XL6?

The 6 seater Maruti XL6 has the dimensions as 4445 x 1775 x 1700 mm. It has a wheelbase of 2740 mm and 180 mm ground clearance. The kerb weight is 1180 kg for Zeta and 1190 kg for Alpha variants. The boot space of XL6 is 209 liters and with 3rd row folded the space increases to 550 liters. Now with both 2nd and 3rd row folded XL6 can have a much spacious boot space as 692 liters. The MPV has got a full tank capacity of 45 liters.

3. Is XL6 is a sedan, SUV or hatchback?

Maruti XL6 is neither hatchback or sedan nor SUV, but its a perfect combination of SUV and MPV vehicles. XL6 has the premium features of an SUV and all the utilities of an MPV. Maruti XL6 is best fitted under the category MUV.

4. What are the fuel options available for Maruti XL6?

Maruti XL6 is powered by a BS6 compliant K15B 1.5-liter engine and Maruti now only offers a petrol engine for the model. Maruti Ertiga uses the same engine and has got a CNG option.

5. What are the safety features of XL6?

Maruti XL6 is based on Maruti’s total effective control technology (ECT) concept. The platform used is the 5th generation of the most successful HEARTECT platform and major body panels and parts are made from high tensile steel for lightweight, better rigidity, and safety. The front dual airbags are a standard safety feature for all four variants of XL6. ISOFIX child seat anchorages - standardized international child seat fitting system for the safety of our kids. ABS with EBD delivers perfect breaking with stability. XL6 comes with seat belt pre-tensioners with force limiters and the combines system delivers better protection. The BS6 compliant SUV has other safety features like anti-theft system, rear washer and defogger, turn indicators on ORVM and parking sensors with camera. In a total XL6 is a premium SUV with best in class safety amenities under 12 lakhs.

6. Does Maruti XL6 have an automatic climate control system?

Yes, Maruti XL6 is furnished with an automatic climate control system. The automatic control system helps to maintain a preferred temperature setting. The system monitors the temperature inside with the previously set standards and sustain the temperature without human intervention.

7. What are the color options available for XL6?

Maruti XL6 is available with 6 color options. The color variants are Nexa Blue, Premium Silver, Magma Grey, Pearl Metallic Auburn Red, Pearl Brave Khaki and Pearl Arctic White.

8. What is the average service cost for Maruti XL6?

The average service charge for Maruti XL6 is around 2400 rupees. Maruti offers the first 3 services free of charge. The customer will not be charged the labor cost and the amount needed will be only for consumables like oils for clutch, brake and engine, oil filters and air filters.
