FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions on Maruti Suzuki Swift 2023



1. Why Maruti Swift is a successful model?

Maruti Suzuki Swift attained a cult image since its launch in 2005. The phenomenal road presence, enviable on-road performance, feature-rich interior, fuel-efficient engine, and admirable styling have been the factors that made Maruti Swift a successful model.

2.How much is a Maruti Swift price in Kerala?

Maruti Swift ex-showroom price varies from 5.92 lakhs to 8.85 lakhs depending on the variant. Call us right away for ascertaining the Maruti Swift on-road price in Kerala for the preferred variant.

3.What is the waiting period for the 2023 Maruti Suzuki Swift?

The waiting period for the 2023 Maruti Suzuki Swift manual is 1 - 2 months and Automatic is 3 - 4 months presently . Observing the booking rate, the waiting period is expected to go up soon.

4.Which trim of Maruti Swift is the best?

Maruti Suzuki Swift ZXi+ variant is considered to be the best one. This top trim has excellent features that make it stand out among the other variants. Automatic LED headlamps, cruise control, colour MID and Auto folding mirrors are a few of the features installed in Maruti Swift ZXi+.

5.Which is better Swift or Baleno?

Both Swift and Baleno are great, feature-rich, cars with excellent performance. Both cars from Maruti Suzuki are selling exceptionally well. The choice of which one to buy would depend solely on the individual.
